Why Choose the Eric Jordan Methodology?

  • Holistic Valuation: Beyond numbers, valuing the heartbeat of your business through the “25 Factors” with emphasis on intangibles like brand, R&D, and customer relations.
  • Practicality: The “5 Senses Inspection Report” provides a tangible grasp of assets’ real‑world value.
  • Cost‑Effectiveness: Comprehensive insights at a fraction of CBV Comprehensive cost, averaging just $3,500.
  • Market Relevance: Aligns with legal requirements of Fair Market Value.
  • Experience‑Driven: Hands‑on operation insights outweigh academic theory, resonating with real business realities.

Professional Business Valuations
