Industry Benchmarks

The entrepreneurial valuator with relevant experience can best determine if industry benchmarks are applicable for a particular situation. Data that is most easily found is for the multi-million dollar players in an industry. However, data gathered from large industry players is seldom applicable to small players. An entrepreneurial valuator is well suited to understand this—there is simply not enough like-for-like data available. We have a link available on data collection comparison.

An experienced valuator can analyze a company's performance against industry benchmarks, providing valuable insights into its relative position and potential for growth.

Meanwhile, an accountant can analyze financial benchmarks, but may not have the operational and industry expertise required to contextualize a company's performance relative to its peers, potentially resulting in an incomplete valuation. Accountants using the income approach without business ownership experience might not contextualize a company's performance relative to industry peers, potentially leading to an incomplete valuation and inaccurate comparisons.
