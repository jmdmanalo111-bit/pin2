Frequently Asked Questions

What does “Fair Market Value” alignment mean?

Reports are structured to the FMV standard, with normalized financials and transparent treatment of both tangible and intangible assets.

How are intangible assets assessed?

Using the 25 Factors framework (e.g., purpose, financials, liquidity, dominance in market) with supporting evidence and inspection notes.

Where does the inspection fit?

The 5 Senses Inspection validates the real-world condition and presentation of assets — an anti-fraud and confidence measure.

Will lenders accept the materials?

Materials are designed to be clear for lenders and legal counsel. Provide your lender’s checklist and we’ll map items directly.
