Auto Repair Shop Example & Industry Categories

Auto Repair Shops (Canada)

Auto Repair Shops: Equipped with hydraulic lifts, diagnostic tools, and specialized repair equipment for servicing vehicles.

This is an asset valuation example for auto repair shop and vehicle maintenance services. A full business valuation can be added.

The purpose could be for sale purposes, financing purposes, divorce, partnership disputes, tax, or other purposes.

Valuation Process

  • Calculate the net proceeds in your balance sheet over and above liabilities.
  • Apply the Eric Jordan “25 Factors Affecting Business Valuation” methodology to find the value of intangible assets.
  • Estimate normalized net income and assign a multiple from the “25 Factors.”
  • Conduct a fair market appraisal on the tools and equipment in a modern repair facility.
  • Determine the value of leasehold improvements.
  • Complete a 5 Senses Inspection Report to support the valuation.

1. 5 Senses Inspection Summary

  • Sight: Bays and equipment functional but show moderate wear. Lighting uneven; signage worn.
  • Sound: Normal operation noise; air compressors slightly loud.
  • Smell: Typical automotive odours present; ventilation could be improved.
  • Touch: Surfaces greasy; bay doors stiff and require lubrication.
  • Taste: Not applicable.

Condition: Fair to Good – Functional, with some areas requiring upgrades or maintenance.

Why Experience Matters

Relevant ownership and operational experience—typically 10–20 years—is essential for determining accurate valuations. Business valuation is both an art and a science that requires hands-on knowledge of operations, not just accounting expertise.
