What is Debt Restructuring?

Debt restructuring is a negotiated adjustment between a debtor and creditors to make obligations bearable while preserving enterprise value. Terms can include reducing principal, extending amortization, or changing interest rates. The goal is to restore financial stability and keep the business operating.
